Weekly Workout Routine

  • Monday: Upper Body Strength and Calisthenics
  • Tuesday: Lower Body Strength and Cardio
  • Wednesday: Active Recovery and Flexibility
  • Thursday: Gymnastics Skills and Upper Body
  • Friday: Lower Body Strength and Cardio
  • Saturday: Full Body and Endurance
  • Sunday: Rest or Light Activity

Monday - Upper Body Strength and Calisthenics

Warm-up (10 minutes)

  • Jumping jacks
  • Arm circles
  • Light jogging

Main Workout

  • Pull-ups: 4 sets of max reps
  • Muscle-ups: Practice technique for 15 minutes
  • Dumbbell bench press: 3 sets of 10 reps with 20 lb dumbbells
  • Dips: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Handstand pushups: 3 sets of max reps

Core Workout

  • Hanging leg raises: 3 sets of 15 reps
  • Planks: 3 sets of 1 minute
  • Russian twists: 3 sets of 20 reps

Cool Down (10 minutes)

  • Stretching exercises focusing on the upper body

Tuesday - Lower Body Strength and Cardio

Warm-up (10 minutes)

  • Light jogging
  • Dynamic stretches

Main Workout

  • Pistol squats: 3 sets of 10 reps each leg
  • Weighted squats (with vest): 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Dumbbell lunges: 3 sets of 15 reps each leg
  • Calf raises: 3 sets of 20 reps

Cardio

  • Run 2 miles at a comfortable pace

Cool Down (10 minutes)

  • Stretching exercises focusing on the lower body

Wednesday - Active Recovery and Flexibility

Flexibility Training (30-45 minutes)

  • Yoga routine focusing on full-body flexibility
  • Stretching for splits and bridges

Core Stability

  • Planches: Practice holds for 15 minutes
  • Front lever practice: 15 minutes

Thursday - Gymnastics Skills and Upper Body

Warm-up (10 minutes)

  • Jumping jacks
  • Arm circles
  • Light jogging

Main Workout

  • Pull-ups: 3 sets of max reps
  • Front lever practice: 15 minutes
  • Planche practice: 15 minutes
  • Dumbbell shoulder press: 3 sets of 12 reps with 20 lb dumbbells
  • Hanging bar hold: 3 sets of 1 minute

Core Workout

  • V-ups: 3 sets of 20 reps
  • L-sit holds: 3 sets of max time
  • Bicycle crunches: 3 sets of 25 reps

Cool Down (10 minutes)

  • Stretching exercises focusing on the upper body

Friday - Lower Body Strength and Cardio

Warm-up (10 minutes)

  • Light jogging
  • Dynamic stretches

Main Workout

  • Pistol squats: 3 sets of 10 reps each leg
  • Weighted lunges (with vest): 3 sets of 15 reps each leg
  • Dumbbell deadlifts: 3 sets of 12 reps with 20 lb dumbbells
  • Glute bridges: 3 sets of 20 reps

Cardio

  • Sprint intervals: 10 x 100m sprints with rest in between

Cool Down (10 minutes)

  • Stretching exercises focusing on the lower body

Saturday - Full Body and Endurance

Warm-up (10 minutes)

  • Jumping jacks
  • Light jogging

Main Workout

  • Circuit training: Repeat 3 times
    • Push-ups: 15 reps
    • Squats: 20 reps
    • Pull-ups: 10 reps
    • Dips: 12 reps
    • Plank: 1 minute

Endurance

  • 7-minute mile test (progress towards goal)

Cool Down (10 minutes)

  • Full-body stretching

Sunday - Rest or Light Activity

Active Recovery

  • Light stretching
  • Easy walk or light yoga
